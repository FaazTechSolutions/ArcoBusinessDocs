# Employee Module

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Employee module is one of the core modules in the HRM (Human Resource Management) system.

This module manages:
- Employee records
- Employee contracts
- Employee profile verification
- Labour compliance
- Payroll grouping
- Government integrations
- Employee assignments

The Employee module acts as the central operational area for managing the complete employee lifecycle.

---

# 3. Employee List

## Purpose

Employee List is the master employee database.

This screen stores:
- Employee details
- Identification numbers
- Arrival information
- Recruitment references

---

## Main Functions

- View all employees
- Search employees
- Select employees
- Access employee operations
- Track employee information

---

## Business Importance

Every employee operation starts from Employee List.

Connected modules:
- HR
- Payroll
- Contract
- Travel
- Finance
- Compliance

# 16. Summary

The Employee module manages the complete employee lifecycle within the HRM system.

It handles:
- Employee records
- Labour contracts
- Payroll grouping
- Government compliance
- Employee assignments

This module is essential for:
- HR operations
- Payroll management
- Saudi labour compliance
- Workforce management

Without this module:
- Employee operations cannot function
- Payroll cannot process
- Labour compliance fails
- Contract assignment becomes impossible

The Employee module acts as the operational backbone of the HRM system.
