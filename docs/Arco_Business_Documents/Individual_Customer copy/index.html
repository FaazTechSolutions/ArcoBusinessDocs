Employee Module

Business Understanding Document​

1. Module Overview

The Employee module is one of the core modules in the HRM (Human Resource Management) system.

This module manages:

  • Employee records
  • Employee contracts
  • Employee profile verification
  • Labour compliance
  • Payroll grouping
  • Government integrations
  • Employee assignments

The Employee module acts as the central operational area for managing the complete employee lifecycle.

3. Employee List

Purpose​

Employee List is the master employee database.

This screen stores:

  • Employee details
  • Identification numbers
  • Arrival information
  • Recruitment references

Main Functions​

  • View all employees
  • Search employees
  • Select employees
  • Access employee operations
  • Track employee information

Business Importance​

Every employee operation starts from Employee List.

Connected modules:

  • HR
  • Payroll
  • Contract
  • Travel
  • Finance
  • Compliance

4. Employee Contract

Purpose​

Employee Contract module manages employment agreements between:

  • Company
  • Employee

This module stores:

  • Employment terms
  • Salary agreements
  • Contract period
  • Renewal details

Main Functions​

FunctionDescription
Create ContractGenerate employee contract
Renew ContractExtend employment
Update TermsModify agreement
Contract TrackingMonitor contract validity

Business Importance​

This module ensures:

  • Legal compliance
  • Labour agreement management
  • Employment tracking

5. Employee Profile Search

Purpose​

Used for advanced employee searching and profile verification.

Users can search employees using:

  • Employee ID
  • Name
  • Iqama Number
  • Passport Number
  • Candidate ID

Main Functions​

  • View detailed employee profile
  • Verify employee records
  • Check employment status
  • Access employee history

Business Importance​

This module helps:

  • HR verification
  • Compliance checking
  • Employee validation

6. Employee Inquiry

Purpose​

Employee Inquiry is used to quickly check employee information without modifying records.

Main Functions​

FunctionDescription
Employee LookupSearch employee information
Status VerificationCheck current employee status
Information ReviewRead-only employee access

Business Importance​

Useful for:

  • HR support teams
  • Customer support
  • Call center operations
  • Compliance verification

7. Mudad Contract Import

What is Mudad?​

Mudad is a Saudi labour and payroll compliance platform.

Purpose​

This module imports labour contract data from Mudad.

Main Functions​

  • Import contract files
  • Sync employee labour contracts
  • Validate imported records

Business Importance​

Ensures:

  • Government compliance
  • Accurate labour contract registration
  • Payroll synchronization

8. Mudad Contract Status

Purpose​

Tracks status of contracts processed through Mudad integration.

Main Functions​

Status TrackingDescription
PendingWaiting for processing
SubmittedSent to Mudad
ApprovedSuccessfully processed
RejectedFailed validation

Business Importance​

Helps organization:

  • Monitor compliance status
  • Resolve integration issues
  • Track labour contract approvals

9. Hourly Assignment

Purpose​

Used to assign employees for temporary or hourly-based work.

Main Functions​

  • Assign hourly tasks
  • Temporary workforce allocation
  • Shift-based assignment

Business Importance​

Useful for:

  • Temporary labour
  • Short-term assignments
  • Flexible workforce management

10. PayGroup Assignment

Purpose​

Employees are grouped for payroll processing.

Main Functions​

FunctionDescription
Assign Pay GroupAdd employee to payroll group
Payroll SegmentationOrganize salary processing
Salary Batch ControlGroup employee salary runs

Business Importance​

Helps payroll team:

  • Process salaries efficiently
  • Separate employee categories
  • Manage payroll structures

11. Qiwa Request

What is Qiwa?​

Qiwa is Saudi Arabia's labour management and compliance platform.

Purpose​

Manage employee-related requests connected with Qiwa system.

Main Functions​

  • Labour transfer requests
  • Employee compliance processing
  • Government approvals
  • Legal labour operations

Business Importance​

Critical for:

  • Saudi labour law compliance
  • Employee legal processing
  • Government integration

Complete Employee Lifecycle​

Recruitment → Candidate Creation → Arrival → Employee Profile → Contract → Payroll → Renewal → Retirement

14. Important Understanding for Freshers

Key Point 1​

Employee module is the core HR operational module.

Key Point 2​

All employee records are centralized here.

Key Point 3​

Government integrations like:

  • Mudad
  • Qiwa

are managed through this module.

Key Point 4​

Employee information flows into:

  • Payroll
  • Contracts
  • Travel
  • Finance
  • Compliance

15. Real Business Example

Example Workflow​

Step 1​

Candidate recruited by recruitment team.

Step 2​

Candidate arrives in Saudi Arabia.

Step 3​

HR creates employee profile.

Step 4​

Employee contract created.

Step 5​

Employee assigned to payroll group.

Step 6​

Employee labour contract synced with Mudad.

Step 7​

Qiwa requests processed for legal compliance.

Step 8​

Employee assigned for work.

This is the complete employee operational lifecycle.

16. Summary

The Employee module manages the complete employee lifecycle within the HRM system.

It handles:

  • Employee records
  • Labour contracts
  • Payroll grouping
  • Government compliance
  • Employee assignments

This module is essential for:

  • HR operations
  • Payroll management
  • Saudi labour compliance
  • Workforce management

Without this module:

  • Employee operations cannot function
  • Payroll cannot process
  • Labour compliance fails
  • Contract assignment becomes impossible

The Employee module acts as the operational backbone of the HRM system.
